Watch It Outside

Boston Edition

- - - - -

This year's edition of the © Watch It Outside film festival will be at Boston Public Garden, from August 5th to August 8th.
There will be many kinds of activities, both during the whole festival and specific activities for themed days. Make sure to check out the about page to learn more about the activities as well as the schedule page for the day-specific activities.

Due to security reasons, the visitors allowance is limited to 1500 people.

    Metropolis (1927)

    August 5th at 8 PM

    DURATION: 1 hr 54 min (114 min)
    - - - - -

    Director: Fritz Lang
    Writers: Thea von Harbou (screenplay), Thea von Harbou (novel)
    Stars: Brigitte Helm, Alfred Abel, Gustav Fröhlich

    Somewhere in the future, Metropolis is a wonderful city. High above the ground its towers stand. The people prosper, the economy is flourishing, the suspended streets are busy.
    But Metropolis also has a great secret. A secret, so well hidden, that not even Freder Fredersen, son of founder John Fredersen knows about it.
    He’s spending his free time in the Eternal Gardens, when suddenly a woman shows up with a group of children. They're from the City of Workers. "These are your brothers", she tells the children, before she is forced to leave.
    Freder is so intrigued that he follows them into the depth. He doesn't like what he sees. The workers are exploited, they must do labor like robots.
    Freder tries to convince his father to change the system, while the woman tries to keep the workers' morale high by predicting a man will stand up that will mediate. Someone that will use both hands and head.
    Someone that will be the heart of Metropolis.

    ABOUT US

    © Watch It Outside Boston Edition is an open air film festival. This year's edition is in Boston Public Garden.

    The festival lasts from the 5th of august until the 8th of August 2019, from 6 PM to midnight. The shows are FREE and open to everyone. Due to security reasons though, the places are limited to 1500 people. This is why we strongly recommend you to fill in our pre-registration form.

    This way, you are sure to claim your spot and experience this never seen before outdoor event with movies and activities that brings together species of all generations and dimensions. Be ready to immerge yourself in an unforgettable experience with the best of the best movie classics in the science-fiction genre.

    Throughtout the whole duration of the festival, theres food stands, a geek photo booth, face make-up artists, vintage/retro art stands, a baby-sitting corner, ‘giving back’ and ‘donations’ booths to help people in need experience entertainment, an 'exchange shop' where you can exchange your collectibles and last but not least, a museum with artifacts used in the movies (where you can later bid on and take them home).

    And that's not all! Every day of the festival, there are day-specific activities you can attend (don't forget to pre-register for these). Wednesday's theme is MONSTERS and on Thursday we organised an EXTRATERRESTRIAL themed day. Be sure to check out the full days schedule to help you decide for your pre-registration.
