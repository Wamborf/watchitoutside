ABOUT US

© Watch It Outside Boston Edition is an open air film festival. This year's edition is in Boston Public Garden.

The festival lasts from the 5th of august until the 8th of August 2019, from 6 PM to midnight. The shows are FREE and open to everyone. Due to security reasons though, the places are limited to 1500 people. This is why we strongly recommend you to fill in our pre-registration form.

This way, you are sure to claim your spot and experience this never seen before outdoor event with movies and activities that brings together species of all generations and dimensions. Be ready to immerge yourself in an unforgettable experience with the best of the best movie classics in the science-fiction genre.

Throughtout the whole duration of the festival, theres food stands, a geek photo booth, face make-up artists, vintage/retro art stands, a baby-sitting corner, ‘giving back’ and ‘donations’ booths to help people in need experience entertainment, an 'exchange shop' where you can exchange your collectibles and last but not least, a museum with artifacts used in the movies (where you can later bid on and take them home).

And that's not all! Every day of the festival, there are day-specific activities you can attend (don't forget to pre-register for these). Wednesday's theme is MONSTERS and on Thursday we organised an EXTRATERRESTRIAL themed day. Be sure to check out the full days schedule to help you decide for your pre-registration.
