Watch It Outside

Boston Edition

This year's edition of the © Watch It Outside film festival will be at Boston Public Garden, from August 5th to August 8th.
There will be many kinds of activities, both during the whole festival and specific activities for themed days. Make sure to check out the about page to learn more about the activities as well as the schedule page for the day-specific activities.

Due to security reasons, the visitors allowance is limited to 1500 people.
